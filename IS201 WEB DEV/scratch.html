Reasons I Like Cars

  1. They are cool to look at.
    • Design, body lines, and details make every model unique.
    • I like noticing the differences between trims and builds.
  2. They sound awesome.
    • Engine tone can completely change the personality of a car.
    • Exhaust notes are honestly one of the best parts.
  3. Cars help me relax and find joy when I am down.
    • Driving clears my head and helps me reset.
    • Learning about cars gives me something fun to focus on.
